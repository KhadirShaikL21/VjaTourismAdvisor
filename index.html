Top Places to Visit in Vijayawada

Prakasam Barrage

An iconic structure over the Krishna River, offering scenic views.

Kanaka Durga Temple

A famous temple dedicated to Goddess Kanaka Durga, offering spiritual experiences.

Bhavani Island

A serene getaway with lush greenery and boating activities.

Gandhi Hill

A memorial for Mahatma Gandhi, offering panoramic views of Vijayawada.

Undavalli Caves

Ancient rock-cut caves with stunning carvings and historical significance.

Amaravati

A historic city known for its Buddhist heritage and ancient stupa.

Rajiv Gandhi Park

A well-maintained park with a mini zoo, perfect for family outings.

Kondapalli Fort

A historic fort with impressive architecture and scenic surroundings.
